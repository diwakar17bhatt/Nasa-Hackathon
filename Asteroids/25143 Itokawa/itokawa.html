25143 Itokawa Asteroid


Discovery

25143 Itokawa was discovered Sept. 26, 1998 by the Lincoln Laboratory Near-Earth Asteroid Research Team at Socorro, New Mexico.

Size

Asteroid 25143 Itokawa is approximately 535 meters by 294 meters by 209 meters in size. It's a sub-kilometer sized, peanut-shaped asteroid with a low density of 1,950 kg/m3.

Distace from Earth

The distance from Earth to the asteroid 25143 Itokawa varies, but its minimum orbital intersection distance is 1,960,000 kilometers, or 5.1 lunar distances. This is the closest point the asteroid will come to Earth.

Velocity of Asteroid

The average orbit speed of asteroid 25143 Itokawa is 25.86 kilometers per second.

Period of Rotation

The rotation period of asteroid 25143 Itokawa is 12.1 hours.

Orbital Period

The orbital period of asteroid 25143 Itokawa is 557 days, or 1.52 years.
