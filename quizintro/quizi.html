Instructions

1. Overview:-
You will be answering a series of 25 multiple-choice questions related to space.
Each question has four answer options (A, B, C, D).
Your goal is to select the correct answer for as many questions as possible!

2. Getting Started:-
Listen Carefully: The quizmaster will read each question aloud. Pay attention!
Take Notes (if necessary): You can note down your thoughts or answer choices if you like.

3. Answering Questions:-
Indicate your answer by: Selecting the appropriate answer.

4. Scoring:-
Each correct answer earns you 1 point. There are no penalties for incorrect answers, so feel free to take a guess!

5. Reviewing Answers:-
Once all questions have been asked, the quizmaster will go over the correct answers.
Keep track of your score as the answers are revealed.

6. Winning:- At the end of the quiz, scores will be tallied. Prizes or certificates may be awarded to the top scorers!

7. Have Fun! Enjoy the quiz and learn some interesting facts about space! Feel free to ask questions if you need clarification on any topics.
